TIEBREAKER PROTOCOL

⚔️ NETHER GOLD RUSH

The two contenders enter the Nether and have 10 minutes to collect as much gold as possible. All sources count — gold ore, nether gold ore, piglin drops, bastion loot. At time's end, the player with the most gold (ingots + nuggets combined, converted to ingot value) wins the round.

Judged and verified on the spot. No inventory swaps after time is called.

🗡️ PURE PVP — LAST MAN STANDING

If any round cannot be decided by scoring: all tied players enter an open fight with the gear they carried. No resets. No second chances. Last surviving player qualifies. Not valid as a tiebreaker in the Final Round.
